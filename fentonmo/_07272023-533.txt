MAYOR'S COMMENTS
Mayor Maurath shared Chuck Mach passed away this past week, he retired after 20 years of service
with the City as a Public Works employee. Prayers and thoughts go out to the families.

Mayor Maurath shared he had attended the Commerce Bank round table discussion this past week.
They provide a blog about community happenings.

Mayor Maurath           shared     the   RiverChase YMCA               has   been    experiencing power outages and                  other
circumstances causing the facility to close. One closure was due to glass found in the pool, which
required the pool to be drained, cleaned, filled and chemicals rebalanced. Power outages were a result
from    lightning strikes on Ameren's poles and main frame damage. The City is                                     looking into      surge
protectors and a generator system.
