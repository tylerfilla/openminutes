REPORTS FROM MAYOR AND COUNCIL
Mayor Counts stated he was pleased with the number of projects that will be completed throughout the City over the course of the summer season. He also encouraged everyone to attend the Farmers Market this Saturday.

EJ Fleischmann, Ward 1 — Stated that residents of Arnold, as well as friends and family who reside outside the city, have told him how much they enjoy Arnold Farmers Market.

Rodney Mullins, Ward 3 — Thanked Judy Wagner for her dedication in applying for available grant funding for various projects, as this saves the City a lot of money. He also thanked Dave Crutchley for his due diligence in making sure repairs are made to the Rec Center.

Rodney Mullins, Ward 3 — Stated the SRO officers are very important and he is pleased we are adding two more.

Brian McArthur, Ward 2 — Stated he wanted to be sure that council was kept updated on the retaining wall project on Church Road.

ADMINISTRATIVE REPORTS
Chief Carroll— Informed council that Officer Ray Hussmann was sworn in today as a new police officer for the City of Arnold.

Dave Crutchley— Encouraged everyone to attend the Fourth of July Celebration, which will be held this year on July 1st.

David Bookless— Informed council that the Comprehensive Plan Workshop dates have been set. Signs will be going up around the city, ads will be placed in The Leader, and the information will be placed on the city website announcing the dates. The first chapter of the Comprehensive Plan has also been uploaded to the website, as well as the informational letter regarding anonymous complaints.
